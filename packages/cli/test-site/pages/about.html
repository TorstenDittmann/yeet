Our Mission

We believe that deploying static sites should be as simple as a single command. Yeet eliminates the friction between development and deployment, letting you focus on what matters most - creating amazing web experiences.

How It Works

Yeet automatically scans your project directory, filters out unnecessary files, and uploads your static assets to our global content delivery network. Within seconds, your site is live and accessible from anywhere in the world.
